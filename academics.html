ACADEMICS AT SAN FRANCISCO HIGH SCHOOL



At San Francisco High School, we pride ourselves on offering a diverse range of tracks, strands, and grade levels to meet
the unique needs and interests of our students. Our curriculum is designed to challenge and inspire students
at every grade level, preparing them for success in higher education and beyond.



Grade Levels Available:

San Francisco High School offers the following grade levels:

  • Grade 7
  • Grade 8
  • Grade 9
  • Grade 10
  • Grade 11
  • Grade 12




Tracks and Strands:

Students at San Francisco High School can choose from a variety of tracks and strands to tailor their academic journey:


  • STEM Track (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) - Prepare for a future in STEM fields.
  • ABM Track (Accountancy, Business, and Management) - Develop skills for the world of business.
  • HUMSS Strand (Humanities and Social Sciences) - Explore social sciences and humanities disciplines.
  • GAS Strand (General Academic Strand) - Build a strong foundation across various subjects.




We also offer TVL Track with these two strands:



  • PROGRAMMING - teaches software development and coding for app creation.
  • ANIMATION - Covers digital animation and graphic design for 2D and 3D creations.
