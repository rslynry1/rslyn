ABOUT SAN FRANCISCO HIGH SCHOOL



Welcome to San Francisco High School, where our students are proudly known as Kikonians!Our mission is to cultivate a supportive and engaging

Explore our state-of-the-art facilities and vibrant campus that foster a conducive learning environment for students to thrive.

preparing them for success in a rapidly changing world.
